About

I'm a product manager who gets a kick out of taking a fuzzy idea, giving it shape with the right team, and watching real people use it.

For the last five years I've built consumer and enterprise products across AI, voice, and content — most recently at LexisNexis, where I lead a multi-region content creation platform and our RAG-based drafting insights. Before that I shipped 0-to-1 voice products at Pucho and prototyped a consumer voice assistant at Vivint.

I like the messy early days the most — user research, rough prototypes, a whiteboard full of question marks. And I love when data and taste agree on what to do next.

Selected work

Four projects I'm especially proud of.

Context

Drafting suggestions were powerful but unreliable — legal users can't afford hallucinations. We needed accuracy that felt trustworthy on the first read.

What I did

  • Partnered with ML to design retrieval over a vetted corpus and iterate on prompt templates.
  • Set up an eval harness with SMEs to score groundedness and tone, separate from engagement metrics.
  • Ran a phased A/B with guardrails on opt-outs and latency.

Outcome

30% engagement lift on drafting surfaces; measurable reduction in hallucinated citations; SMEs moved from skeptics to promoters.

Context

Creators juggled four tools to draft a single piece of content. I owned the roadmap to unify the workflow into one lightweight experience.

What I did

  • Ran research (surveys, click tests, interviews) across 5 personas to map the core job.
  • Shipped from concept → beta → GA across two years, with A/B tests at every meaningful step.
  • Built a KPI dashboard with data science tracking 15+ adoption and engagement metrics.

Outcome

Monthly actives grew 55% to 5,000+; creation time dropped 25%; 50%+ feature adoption within 3 months.

Context

Non-technical users in regional India were being left out of digital content. I led a voice-first platform built for them from day one.

What I did

  • Ran field research and usability sessions in Hindi, Marathi, and Kannada to define the MVP.
  • Worked with design and engineering on an intuitive voice grammar and tight iteration loops.
  • Shipped weekly based on live analytics and WhatsApp user feedback.

Outcome

15,000+ users in the first three months across three languages; strong validation for voice-first consumer products in low-bandwidth markets.

Context

Visitors at a 50k-daily-footfall mall needed quick, multilingual wayfinding and store info — no app download, no learning curve.

What I did

  • Scoped an in-kiosk voice experience with the UX team that worked for non-technical users.
  • Defined success metrics and ran the pilot with clear go/no-go criteria.
  • Coordinated the client and engineering to ship a working kiosk in weeks.

Outcome

Live voice queries for 50k daily visitors; validated voice-first interaction in a high-noise public space.
